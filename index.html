Emergency SOS — Privacy Policy

✅ No data sold. No tracking. Privacy first.

1. What We Collect

Guardian X collects only what is necessary for emergency safety features:

  • Location (GPS) — only when SOS is triggered or live tracking is active. Never collected silently in the background.
  • Emergency contacts — names and phone numbers you enter manually. Stored only on your device.
  • Device sensors — accelerometer (shake detection), compass/magnetometer (compass feature). Never transmitted.
  • Anonymous ad data — Google AdMob collects anonymous ad interaction data per Google's own privacy policy.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or account details
  • We do not store your location on any server
  • We do not read your SMS history, call logs, or phone contacts beyond what you manually enter
  • We do not sell any data to any third party
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites

3. How We Use Location

Location is used exclusively to:

  • Include your GPS coordinates in emergency SOS messages sent by SMS to your chosen contacts
  • Generate a live tracking link (Ultra Pro feature) shared only with your emergency contacts when SOS is triggered
  • Detect your country automatically to show the correct emergency service numbers

Location is never uploaded to our servers. The live tracking link encodes your coordinates directly in the URL — no backend database stores this data. When your contact opens the link, their browser calculates the distance locally.

4. Live Tracker Feature (Ultra Pro)

When SOS is triggered with live tracking enabled, a URL is generated containing your GPS coordinates, your name, and your phone number. This URL is sent via SMS to your emergency contacts only. No server stores this data. The link uses OpenStreetMap to display the map — no Google account or API key is required.

5. Permissions Explained

  • SEND_SMS — to send emergency SOS alerts to your contacts
  • CALL_PHONE — to call emergency contacts and services directly
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION — to include GPS in emergency messages
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE — to keep shake-to-SOS active when app is in background
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — to restart shake monitoring after phone reboot
  • CAMERA — to activate flashlight SOS strobe signal
  • VIBRATE — for SOS vibration pattern and Morse code
  • READ_CONTACTS — only to let you pick a contact from your phonebook; not stored elsewhere

6. Third Party Services

  • Google AdMob — serves banner and rewarded ads. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing — handles in-app purchases. No payment data is processed by Guardian X directly.
  • OpenStreetMap — used in the live tracking web page to display maps. No personal data is shared with OpenStreetMap.

7. Data Retention

All data (emergency contacts, settings, notes) is stored only on your device using Android SharedPreferences. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all local data. We retain nothing on servers because Guardian X has no servers.

8. Children's Privacy

Guardian X is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Your Rights

You can delete all app data at any time: Android Settings → Apps → Guardian X → Storage → Clear Data. You can revoke any permission: Android Settings → Apps → Guardian X → Permissions.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The latest version will always be available at this URL. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
