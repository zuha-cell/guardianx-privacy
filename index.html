🛡️ Privacy Policy

Guardian X — SOS

Developed by: MZN Digital
Package: com.mzndigital.guardianxsos
Last updated: March 2026

Short version: We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data. Your information stays on your device and is only sent directly to the emergency contacts you choose.

1. Introduction

MZN Digital ("we", "our", "us") built the Guardian X — SOS mobile application ("the App"). This privacy policy explains how the App handles information when you use it. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information the App Uses

The App uses the following information only on your device:

  • Location (GPS) — Your GPS coordinates are used to build the SOS alert message. Location data is sent directly from your device to your saved emergency contacts via SMS. It is not transmitted to any server operated by us.
  • Contacts — The App reads your phone contacts only when you choose to select an emergency contact. Contact data is stored locally on your device only. We do not access or store your full contact list.
  • Phone calls — The App can make phone calls to your saved emergency contacts or local emergency services (Police/Ambulance/Fire) when you activate SOS. No call data is transmitted to us.
  • SMS — The App sends SMS messages to your saved emergency contacts during SOS activation. Messages contain your name, phone number, and GPS location. These are sent from your own SIM — we do not see or store them.
  • Camera / Flash — Used only for the Morse SOS strobe feature (flash the camera LED). No photos or video are taken or stored.
  • Microphone — Not used.
  • Storage — App settings (language, contact names, preferences) are stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. Nothing is uploaded to external servers.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not operate any server that receives your data
  • We do not collect analytics about your usage
  • We do not collect your name, email, or account information
  • We do not track your location in the background beyond what you explicitly enable
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade any data to third parties
  • No account or registration is required to use the App

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can review Google's privacy policy at: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play Services — Used for location detection and app distribution. Subject to Google's terms: policies.google.com/privacy

5. Permissions Explained

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION — To get your GPS coordinates for SOS alerts
  • ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION — To continue sending location updates during Live Tracking even when the screen is off
  • READ_CONTACTS — To let you pick emergency contacts from your phone book
  • SEND_SMS — To send SOS alert messages to your emergency contacts
  • CALL_PHONE — To make direct emergency calls without opening the dialler
  • CAMERA — To use the flashlight for Morse SOS signal
  • VIBRATE — For Morse SOS vibration pattern
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — To show SOS active notification (Android 13+)
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — To restart shake detection after device reboot
  • REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS — To ensure SOS features work in background

6. Children's Privacy

This App is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

7. Data Security

Since no personal data is transmitted to our servers, data security risks to us are minimal. Your emergency contact information and preferences stored locally on your device are protected by Android's app sandboxing. We recommend securing your device with a PIN or biometric lock.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect your personal data, there is no stored data to access, correct, or delete on our end. All App data (contacts, preferences, settings) can be deleted by uninstalling the App from your device.
